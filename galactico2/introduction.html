THE ASSIGNMENTS

A few words about the heart of The Galactico Method – the assignments.Each assignment is broken down into different steps.

You may find it helpful to read through the steps of an assignment before you begin it, to prepare for what it requires from you.

Each assignment has a guideline completion time marked alongside its title.

The guideline completion time of an assignment is only an indication of how long it will take. Work at your own pace.

Most of the assignments require you to write something down.

Writing is an essential part of The Galactico Method process: it will help you to remember your responses, think clearly and be creative.

Throughout the course you will find suggestions as to how you might answer questions posed in the assignments. These suggestions will often be made by Galactico Gus or Galactico Gal.

Use these suggestions as inspiration for your own answers, but it is important to think for yourself and respond authentically. .

As long as your responses are genuine, there is no such thing as a wrong answer.
