S Assignment 1A

Your values are the things that are most important to you in life. When you live according to them you are more inspired, energised and creative.

Within each area of life you also have a set of values, things that matter more to you about a particular subject than anything else. Identifying what you value most about football holds the key to unlocking your greatest potential as a player.

Aligning your footballing ambitions and activities with what you value most about the game will activate your inner-genius as a player.

You’ll be sharper, more confident and more dynamic.

It often happens that we adopt another person’s values as our own, particularly those of individuals we respect and admire. However, someone else’s values are based on what is important to them and not necessarily what matters to you.

When you play football to fulfil what you value most about the game you are much more likely to flourish.

Objective: Set a long-term footballing goal.

STEP ONE

Think about what you would love to accomplish as a footballer.

Perhaps you want to shine in a five-a-side match or be fit enough to last a full ninety minutes.Maybe you want to be offered a place at the Ajax Youth Academy or win the Ballon D’Or

STEP TWO

Choose a footballing goal you would love to accomplish that will take a minimum of one year to achieve.

Be as ambitious as you can but you must believe the goal you choose is possible; a goal you don’t believe you can accomplish will eventually become a burden.A goal you really want to achieve and you believe is possible will inspire you to realise it.

STEP THREE

Is the goal you have chosen aligned with your Galactico Values?

Check that it is by asking yourself “Will achieving this goal help me to fulfil my Galactico Values”? If the answer is yes, continue on to STEP FOUR. Otherwise, go back to STEP TWO and reimagine your goal.

STEP FOUR

Complete the statement below with the details of your long-term footballing goal.

For example, “It is 1st June 2022. I am a professional footballer and I have signed a three-year contract with AC Milan.”.Defining a target completion date helps you to focus, plan and measure progress. Stating the goal in the present tense, as if it has been achieved, affirms it is possible and activates a winning state of mind within you.

Congratulations, you have set your Galactico Mission! Like any goal you set it can be updated whenever you think it could be made more exciting or authentic.

SHORT-TERM GOALS

Your Galactico Mission will be easier to achieve when you break it down into more manageable steps. Each step can be a goal in itself, a short-term goal that, once achieved, moves you closer to success with your greater ambitions.
