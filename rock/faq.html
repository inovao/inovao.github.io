Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Rock Star Method?

The Rock Star Method is a unique online course that uses personal transformation assignments to empower you as a guitarist.

Is the System for Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced Players?

By empowering you from within, The Rock Star Method is designed to advance you as a guitarist whether you are a complete beginner, a virtuoso, or anywhere in-between.

Is the System for Acoustic or Electric Guitar?

It’s perfect for both. The techniques and principles in The Rock Star Method apply to every type of guitar.

Is the System for Guitarists who play Rock only?

The techniques and principles in The Rock Star Method apply to every style of guitar playing, from lead and rhythm to blues, jazz, classical, metal, punk and everything else. As long as you are a guitarist who wants to shine, The Rock Star Method is for you.

Does the System Include Guitar Lessons?

The Rock Star Method does not provide traditional lessons on how to play songs, make chord shapes, etc. It provides personal transformation assignments that will fast-track your development by activating states of mind and ways of thinking that bring out your best as a guitarist.

Who Created The Rock Star Method?

The Rock Star Method was created by fully qualified performance coach Chris Nolan. You can learn more about him at chrisnolancoach.com.
