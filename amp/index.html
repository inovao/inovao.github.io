What is Purpurex?

Purpurex is a patented, breakthrough nutritional supplement for bruising developed by distinguished Palm Beach dermatologist Dr. Joshua Berlin. It’s a clinically proven formulation that contains 6 bruise fighting natural ingredients that are safe, non-toxic and doctor recommended for men and women that are experiencing Solar or Senile Purpura. At present, Purpurex is the ONLY nutritional supplement that is clinically proven to reduce Purpura related bruising.

How Does it Work?

Purpura is thought to be caused by an increased weakening in the connective tissues and blood vessels in those over the age of 50. The unique combination of ingredients found in Purpurex is believed to reduce capillary fragility, build collagen, and maintain capillary strength. In fact, an independent clinical study with 67 participants showed an average 50% reduction in bruising for participants that took Purpurex daily.

REAL Clinical Data

Supplements are considered by many to be the Wild West of American health. Most have little to no clinical evidence, and some may do more harm than good. Purpurex is different. We conducted a multicenter, randomized, placebo-controlled, double-blind study that enrolled 70 patients diagnosed with Purpura. Patients were randomized to receive either Purpurex, or placebo twice daily in oral form for six weeks. At the six-week follow up, a 50 percent reduction in the purpura lesions from baseline was seen in those patients receiving Purpurex, compared to a 9 percent increase in purpura lesions in the placebo group. Purpurex contains a highly sensitive and patented blend of herbal ingredients that are sourced and manufactured according to the highest quality standards.

Citrus Bioflavonoids: Bioflavonoids were first discovered in 1936 by Nobel-prize winning scientist and vitamin C research pioneer Albert Szent-Gyorgi, who originally named the group of compounds "vitamin P." 3 Since then, thousands of flavonoids have been identified and classified according to chemical structure. Bioflavonoids have strong antioxidant properties and appear to act synergistically with vitamin C to neutralize free radicals. They are thought to be particularly beneficial for capillary strength, possibly by helping protect collagen, and they may also promote healthy circulation, as well as immune, cognitive and joint health.

Rutin: Rutin is another type of bioflavonoid, or plant pigment, that is found in certain vegetables and fruits. Apples are full of rutin. Buckwheat, most citrus, figs, and both black and green tea also contain rutin. Rutin has powerful antioxidant properties. It also helps your body produce collagen and use vitamin C.

Ascorbic Acid (a.k.a vitamin C): Ascorbic acid is an antioxidant that provides lots of important nutritional benefits—like boosting your immune system and preventing heart disease and eye problems. It also is believed to combat sun damage, reduce inflammation, brighten pigmentation, and stimulate new and healthy collagen formation in skin. In essence, consuming the proper amount of vitamin C can make your skin look A LOT better. When the proper amount and quality of this wonder-ingredient is combined with the Purpurex formulation, it’s a homerun for bruising.

Hesperidin: Hesperidin is a compound in orange peels that gives the flavonoid hesperitin to the body, and this flavonoid mediates most benefits of hesperidin including an increase in circulation. It is commonly used for blood vessel conditions such as hemorrhoids, varicose veins, and poor circulation (venous stasis). Hesperidin may help blood vessels function better. It may also reduce inflammation.

Eriocitrin: Eriocitrin (eriodictyol 7‐O‐β‐rutinoside) is the main flavonoid in lemon fruit and of all the bioactive molecules of avlemon, eriocitrin is believed to have the most antioxidant activity. As with other bioflavonoids, it is thought to be particularly beneficial for capillary strength, and helping to protect collagen.

Arnica Montana: Arnica Montana is one of the most beneficial substances available in nature’s pharmacopeia, with the power to relieve many of the common aches, pains, and injuries that arise in our daily lives. Arnica is one of the best remedies available to heal bruises faster. When blood vessels break under the skin, causing discoloration, arnica montana speeds up the healing process, prompting the body to send more white blood cells to clean up and repair the bruising.
