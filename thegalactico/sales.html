Why I Created This Course

From: Chris Nolan, Creator of The Galactico Method

If you’re like me then you love the guitar.

But improving is tough. There’s mistakes, inconsistency, criticism, slow progress, criticism and not having the right people around you to contend with. And that’s just for starters.

Unless, of course, you’re a rock star. From the outside looking in they appear to progress quickly, play confidently and write original songs effortlessly. Is it because they have a natural advantage, or is there something else going on?

I started to research how the greatest players made it to the top. And it soon became clear that what separates them from other guitarists is their state of mind: their focus, their self-belief, their vision, how they react to setbacks, what they tell themselves, those types of things.

Now, I’ve been studying personal development for over 20 years - and I realized that I had the tools and techniques which, if I adapted them to playing guitar, could install these qualities and create a Rock Star state of mind.

So, that’s exactly what I did! And the result is The Rock Star Method.

After applying the tools in The Rock Star Method, my own guitar development went into overdrive: I went from messing around at home to being the lead guitarist in a band and playing live around the world. I wrote the songs I’d always wanted to and enjoyed making music like never before.

I took my guitar-playing clients through the process and watched beginners who could barely play a scale lay down killer solos; I saw experienced players overcome nerves to blow audiences away onstage and I saw experts caught in plateaus smash through them to reach new levels of excellence.

Because they had gotten their mind-sets right, they became unstoppable.

And now, you have access to these tools in The Rock Star Method. There's nothing else like it out there - and I guarantee that if you’re serious about achieving your goals with the guitar, it’s going to be one of the best investments you ever make.
