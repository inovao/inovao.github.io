Why I Created This Course

From: Chris Nolan, Creator of The Galactico Method



If you’re like me then you love playing football.

But improving as a player is tough. There’s nerves, inconsistency, criticism and not having the right people around you to contend with. And that’s just for starters.

I started to research how the greatest players overcame these challenges and made it to the top. And it soon became clear that it was down to their state of mind: their focus, their self-belief, their vision, how they reacted to setbacks, what they told themselves, etc.

Now, I’ve been studying personal development for over 20 years - and I realized that I had the tools and techniques which, if I adapted them to playing football, could install these qualities and create a Galactico* state of mind.

So, that’s exactly what I did! And the result is The Galactico Method.

After applying the tools in The Galactic Method, my own game went over the top: I went from messing around with friends in the park to being a first choice midfielder for clubs in England, Australia and Scotland. I had the fitness, confidence and vision I’d always wanted, and I enjoyed playing like never before.

I took my footballer clients through the process and watched beginners play killer passes and create game-changing moments; I saw experienced players overcome nerves to dictate matches and I saw experts stuck in ruts smash through them to reach new levels of excellence.

Because they had gotten their mind-sets right, they became unstoppable.

These tools are available to you now in The Galactico Method. It’s a completely unique system that - if you want to make your mark as a player - is going to be one of the best investments you ever make.

*Galactico (noun) - An exceptionally skilled and celebrated soccer player.
