ED Medications

Our Rex MD physicians can help diagnose and treat your ED symptoms with FDA-approved medications from the comfort of your own home.

You’re probably familiar with the most well-known ED treatments Viagra (generic name sildenafil), Cialis (generic name tadalafil), and Levitra (generic name vardenafil). What you may not know is that these medications are now available in a variety of forms and prices.

We’re fans of transparency, so here’s a breakdown of Rex MD’s available medications and pricing. *Member pricing may vary based on your refill regularity and membership plan.

Generic Viagra (Sildenafil)

Generic Sildenafil 20mg sildenafil for ED is available as an off-label use of the medication, and it’s up to the medical judgment of your doctor to decide if this treatment is appropriate. This drug was originally approved as Revatio (sildenafil 20mg) for pulmonary arterial hypertension. It’s widely used, however, for the treatment of ED, just with a different dosing strategy. In this case, each dose is made up of one or multiple 20mg pills. For instance, a 100mg dose requires five 20mg pills. This is how many of our competitors sell sildenafil. Fortunately for you, our simple and convenient dosing require just ONE pill. Why take five?
