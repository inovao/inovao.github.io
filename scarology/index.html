General Questions

Why is Scarology better than other products on the market?

Scarology was created by Dr. Michael Connor and Dr. Jeanmarie Connor because the commercially available scar treatment products they were recommending to their patients did not convincingly show any improvement in the appearance of scars. Scarology’s complete scar treatment system is different than any scar treatment products available on the market today, many of which have been shown in scientific studies to have little to no benefit. Scarology is an innovation in scar treatment combining exfoliation, a scar cream and silicone sheets all within a simple day and night routine.

No other scar treatment has incorporated a gentle exfoliation step to aid in the prevention of new scars and in remodeling old scars. Exfoliation procedures such as dermabrasion or laser resurfacing have been shown to be effective in improving the appearance of a scar but can cost thousands of dollars with the possibility of significant down time. Scarology’s Natural Fruit Acid Skin Exfoliator works on the scar over time and is easily incorporated into your daily routine.

Scarology’s proprietary scar cream was formulated after extensive research of the medical literature. The goal was to create a scar cream with natural ingredients and to avoid using ingredients that have been scientifically shown to have little or no benefit, such as onion extract. Some of the ingredients, until now, have only been available in medical settings and are not currently in any other over the counter scar creams. These key ingredients have been shown to nourish the healing scar with hydration, boost the immune system, and reduce inflammation in the scar tissue*.
