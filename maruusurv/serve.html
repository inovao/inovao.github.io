Self Serve

A simple interface to allow you to create your own end to end research

Create your survey, select your audience, indicate whether it's a "one off" or whether it should run repeatedly (and how often). Then just run the survey and watch the first results roll in within seconds

Sign up & Get Started Schedule a Demo

How it works?

  • Create your questions

    Creating a survey requires no experience and can be done within minutes.

  • Select Your Audience

    If you have a particular audience in mind for your research, you can target them here.

  • Scheduling and Tracking

    If you want your survey to run at a particular time (after an event for instance), or you want it to run regularly (perhaps to track customer sentiment?), you can do that evry easily here.

  • Review Your Results

    We'll check your survey after you submit it. If we have any recomendations we'll pss it back to you with advice. Otherwise it will go live and you will receive your first results within minutes.
