PR Agency 1 - Case study

Who uses surveys? – Always On

PR Agency One is an award winning PR agency, working for a range of clients each with very differing needs. “A one size approach certainly never fits our clients”, said James Crawford MD. “When we looked for a research firm to conduct omnibus research, we didn’t want one that ran to a fixed schedule at specific times and took days to report back the findings. Usurv has been incredibly flexible, always on and has enabled our agency to run surveys amongst different respondent groups, with different sample sizes and get back findings at any time and on any day of the week”.
Findings from Usurv projects are available instantly online via the client dashboard with all the tools built in for sophisticated analysis.

help

The Issue

Fast, flexible research is a rare commodity. Omnibus research, where a few questions can be run exists in abundance in the UK, but most omnibuses run to a schedule and the schedule includes date / time of fielding as well as who (and how many respondents) to interview.

check_circle

The Solution

PR Agency One, an award-winning PR agency, working for a range of clients each with very differing needs was looking for a research agency.
“When we looked for a research firm to conduct omnibus research, we didn’t want one that ran to a fixed schedule at specific times and took days to report back the findings. maru/usurv has been incredibly flexible, always on and has enabled our agency to run surveys amongst different respondent groups, with different sample sizes and get back findings at any time and on any day of the week”.

stars

The Outcome

Research can be run, whenever, with whoever and is cost effective, easy to set up and always on. Want research Wednesday at 6:00pm for a Thursday pitch – no problem. Need 1,000 respondents fast – done. Only interested in females – it’s just a click.
“Now we can do research whenever we need to and insight informs what we do”.
