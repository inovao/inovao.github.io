Help for older people to stay healthy and active.

Join a planned activity group which aims to maintain an older person to continue living at home and in the community.

Our Activity Groups offer a planned program of structured activities that also provide respite and support for carers.​

Some of these activities include:

  • - Social events – such as Men’s Shed, knitting groups, themed events/parties or visitors from the local community or schools

  • - Entertainment – such as movies, performers, speakers and presenters

  • - Games – crosswords and quizzes, cards, board games and bingo

  • - Outdoor activities – such as walking groups, bus outings and more.

This service will help you with:

  • ​- Promotion of independence by supporting and maintaining the individual’s ability to continue to live at home and be included in their community

  • - Social interaction and friendship

  • - Support with making connections with other agencies

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the service cost?

There is a small fee for some of our services. Please call us on 1300 003 224 for more info.

Who can use this service?

People over 65 years and over (50 years and over for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people) who live in Melbourne or Tasmania.

Do I need a referral?

Yes, you will need to contact My Aged Care first to access this service.

Is there transport available?

Transport may be available subject on needs and mobility assessment, availability and geographic location.

Can I pick and choose which activities I want to be involved in?

Yes. You can choose which activities you want to be involved in. There are a variety of social groups operating throughout the week.
