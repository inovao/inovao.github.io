NeuroCap™

NeuroCap™ is a disposable, pre-gelled, and standard 10-20 electrode system headset that can be applied easily and quickly.

  • Simple Design: Can be put on by any layperson
  • Disposable Cap: Safe, comfortable, and sanitary
  • Quick Setup: Takes less than 5 minutes
  • Compatible: Works with other EEG devices

NeuroEEG™

NeuroEEG™ is a modern, portable EEG system that fits in the palm of your hand.

  • Simple Design: Can be put on by any layperson
  • Disposable Cap: Safe, comfortable, and sanitary
  • Quick Setup: Takes less than 5 minutes

With hospital Emergency Departments' being open around the clock, only 2% are actually equipped with EEG machines or staffed with technologists and neurohospitalists to retrieve quality EEG recordings.
